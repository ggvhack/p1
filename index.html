How it works

Simple to join.
Powerful together.

In three easy steps, you go from curious bystander to active community hero.

1

Create your account

Sign up as a Volunteer, NGO, or Organisation. Set your city and get instantly connected to your local community.

2

Join the conversation

Browse the community feed, discover volunteer drives near you, and post your own initiatives to recruit help.

3

Create real impact

Earn points on the leaderboard, support donation campaigns, and track how your contributions change lives.

Who's it for

Built for every part of the ecosystem

Whether you have two free hours or run a full NGO — CareConnect has a place for you.

Volunteers

Individuals who want to give back. Find opportunities that match your schedule, skills, and passion.

  • Browse nearby drives
  • Earn points & climb leaderboard
  • Post updates & inspire others
  • Access emergency guides

NGOs

Registered organisations managing campaigns and coordinating volunteers across the region.

  • Launch donation campaigns
  • Recruit volunteers at scale
  • Build community trust
  • Share verified updates

Organisations

Corporates, institutions, and groups looking to contribute resources and coordinate CSR activities.

  • Sponsor campaigns
  • Coordinate team volunteering
  • Gain community visibility
  • Transparent impact tracking

Features

Everything you need to coordinate good

Community Feed

A live social feed of volunteer activities, campaign updates, and calls to action from your city.

💰

Donation Campaigns

Transparent, targeted campaigns with real-time progress tracking for causes that need funding.

🏆

Leaderboard

Gamified recognition to celebrate top volunteers and keep the community motivated.
