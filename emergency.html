Quick First Aid

CPR

30 chest compressions, then 2 rescue breaths. Push hard and fast — at least 2 inches deep. Repeat until help arrives.

Bleeding

Apply firm pressure with a clean cloth. Do not remove the cloth. Elevate the limb above heart level if possible.

Burns

Cool with running water for 10–20 minutes. Do not apply ice or butter. Cover loosely with a clean bandage.
